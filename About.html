About Us


“The way to get started is to take the first step with dream-powered optimism.”


Established by The Students' Union, SRCC in the year 2016, "With Love" is an initiative to mobilize the citizens of the country especially the students, towards the greater good of the society. It strives to undertake a nation wide campaign every year to solve various issues that plague the society.
