Spread The Warmth


In December 2016, a poignant need that exists pan India was identified and “Spread the Warmth” was conceived. Over a span of 15 days, the team collected 4.68 tonnes of warm clothes across seven states in India. The same was distributed to the needy in collaboration with the Future Group (Big Bazaar), Safexpress and Goonj NGO, thereby saving 25000 lives from succumbing to the shivering cold.

Cities Covered

  • Delhi
  • Mumbai
  • Bangalore
  • Chennai
  • Kolkata
  • Patna
  • Siliguri

Collection Mechanism

  1. COLLECTION CENTRES: 74 retail outlets of Big Bazaar were set up as collection centres across the seven selected cities.
  2. SCHOOLS AND COLLEGES: Various schools and colleges conducted the collection drive in their premises to ensure maximum collection from the people.
  3. DOORSTEP COLLECTION: People from across the city voluntarily came together to conduct doorstep collection in various residential areas.

Total Collections

  • The collection from the Big Bazaar outlets was 8.12 Quintals
  • The doorstep collection which was conducted in Delhi and Siliguri was 20.52 Quintals of clothes in just 12.5 hours.
  • The collection from a schools and colleges was 16.20 Quintals.
  • The total collection from the drive in just 15 days amounted to 4.48 tonnes.
