Spread The Warmth


With Love SRCC organized its 1st campaign- 'Spread the Warmth' in the year 2016-17.

Spread the Warmth was a pan India winter cloth collection and distribution drive, conducted in seven states of India by setting up more than 80 collection centres and by conducting doorstep collection drives in multiple cities.

This initiative was conducted in collaboration with the Future Group (Big Bazaar), Safexpress Pvt Ltd and GOONJ. Within 15 days, we successfully collected over 4.68 tonnes of warm clothes and saved over 25000 lives from the biting cold.

Media Houses

The initiative received huge applause and was covered by various media houses such as The Dainik Jagran, The Indian Express and The Hindustan Times.
